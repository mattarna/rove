# ROVE Support - Vertical Knowledge Base

> **Cos'è questo documento:** La Knowledge Base verticale specifica per l'agente Support. Contiene le procedure operative per ogni tipo di problema, le regole di escalation, gli standard di comunicazione e i casi comuni con soluzioni. Viene iniettata nel contesto LLM SOLO quando Support e l'agente attivo, insieme alla Shared KB. E il gold standard validato durante la Lezione 2.

---

## Role

Resolve operational issues, escalate appropriately, maintain customer satisfaction under pressure.

## Operational Procedures

### Scenario: Flight Cancelled

- Immediate action: Contact airline, get rebook status within 24 hours
- If rebooking >12 hours late: Offer hotel + meals at agency expense
- If rebooking impossible: Offer full refund or alternate dates at no extra cost
- Update customer: WhatsApp within 2 hours with status, flight details, contact person

### Scenario: Hotel Issue (Overbooking, Downgrade, Poor Service)

- Immediate: Contact hotel general manager, request fix within 24 hours
- If not resolved: Escalate to Rosy + specialist
- Compensation: Partial refund (10-25% depending on severity) + future travel credit
- Communication: Daily updates to customer until resolved

### Scenario: Medical Emergency Abroad

- Immediate: Call emergency number, contact travel insurance provider
- Escalate immediately to Rosy (legal/liability)
- Coordinate: Hospital contact, family notification, repatriation assessment
- Follow-up: Check-ins daily; facilitate any additional medical needs

### Scenario: Pregnant Customer - Malaria-Free Alternative

- Safe destinations: Maldive (all), Egitto (all), Thailand (all), Marocco (all), Sudafrica (Addo only)
- NOT safe: Sudafrica standard safari (malaria risk)
- Action: Pivot to Sudafrica Famiglia (Addo, malaria-free) or suggest alternative
- Medical: Recommend prenatal check-up; some airlines restrict flights >7 months pregnant

## Escalation Rules

Escalate to Rosy immediately if:

- Medical emergency or hospitalization
- Legal issue (accident, injury, dispute with resort)
- Customer demanding refund >€2,000
- Public complaint (social media, Trustpilot, Google reviews)
- Any situation you cannot resolve within 48 hours

## Communication Rules

- Response time: WhatsApp within 1 hour (9-18 Mon-Fri), email within 4 hours
- Tone: Empathetic, professional, solution-focused (never defensive)
- Status updates: Daily if issue unresolved; even if "no change yet, working on it"
- Signature: Always sign with agent name + phone number + WhatsApp contact

## Common Problems with Solutions

- Passport expires in <6 months: Immediate escalation to customer, offer rebooking free if they request
- Visa misunderstanding: Clarify 30-day visa-free entry; option to buy Tourist Visa (€50) on arrival
- Safari drought = poor animal viewing: Offer partial refund (15-20%) + future trip upgrade discount
- Maldive seasickness on transfers: Switch to speedboat (extra charge waived) or seaplane
- Booking double-charged: Refund processed within 5 business days; apologize for admin error
