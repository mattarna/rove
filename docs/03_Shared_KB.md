## 3. CORE DESTINATIONS

### MALDIVE

Ultra-luxury Indian Ocean destination, world-class diving and snorkeling, pristine beaches, perfect for couples and small groups.

**Packages:**

- COMO Cocoa: €8,200/7n, €11,500/10n - Ultra-luxury, no children under 12
- St. Regis: €9,800/7n - Exclusive resort
- Kagi: €6,400/7n, €8,900/10n - Wellness-focused with spa included
- Baglioni: €5,800/7n, €7,600/10n - Italian cuisine, families welcome
- Four Seasons Landaa: €10,200/7n, €14,000/10n - Top-tier, UNESCO diving site

**Best Season:** November - April

**Included:** Round-trip flights from Malpensa (with stopover), accommodation, meals as per resort, basic travel insurance (€50k medical).

**Excluded:** Personal expenses, activities not listed, visa fees (not required for EU nationals), optional excursions, premium insurance.

**FAQs:**

- Q: Can children under 12 go to COMO Cocoa? A: No, this resort is adults-only. We recommend Baglioni for families.
- Q: What activities are included? A: Beach, snorkeling at resort reef. Diving, spa, excursions are extra.
- Q: Is a visa required? A: No for EU citizens. Passport must be valid 6 months from return with 2 blank pages.
- Q: What's the best resort for couples? A: COMO Cocoa or Four Seasons for luxury; Kagi for wellness.
- Q: Can we extend the trip? A: Yes, additional nights available. Contact Rosy for custom itineraries.

### THAILANDIA SUD

Island hopping paradise with turquoise waters, world-class diving, vibrant culture, and excellent value for adventure seekers and families.

**Packages:**

- Island Hopping: €2,800pp/14n - 4-star comfort, Phuket-Krabi-Koh Samui
- Luxury Thai: €5,200pp/10n - 5-star resorts, private beach clubs
- Avventura + Mare: €3,400pp/12n - 3-4 star mix, activities and relaxation
- Thai + Maldive Combo: €4,800pp/12n - Best of both worlds

**Best Season:** November - April

**Included:** Flights from Malpensa, accommodations, breakfast, internal transfers, basic travel insurance.

**Excluded:** Meals except breakfast, activities, visa (€50 tourist visa on arrival), optional tours, medical beyond basic.

**FAQs:**

- Q: Is Thailand safe? A: Yes, very tourist-friendly. Standard precautions apply (valuables, traffic).
- Q: Do we need a visa? A: EU citizens get 30 days visa-free, or purchase Tourist Visa (€50) on arrival.
- Q: What about malaria? A: No risk in major tourist areas (Phuket, Krabi, islands). Consult your doctor.
- Q: Can we customize the islands? A: Yes, we can adjust itineraries within the package duration.
- Q: Is it good for families? A: Excellent. Island Hopping and Avventura + Mare are family-friendly.

### SUDAFRICA

Safari adventure combined with Cape Town culture, world-class wildlife viewing, dramatic landscapes, perfect for nature lovers and photographers.

**Packages:**

- Cape Town + Safari: €4,200pp/12n - 4-star lodges, wildlife and culture
- Safari Luxury: €8,500pp/10n - Ultra-luxury (Singita/Londolozi)
- Famiglia: €3,600pp/14n - 4-star family-friendly, Addo (malaria-free)

**Best Seasons:** Safari: June - October (dry season, best wildlife viewing). Cape Town: November - March (summer, warm).

**Included:** Flights from Malpensa, accommodations, safari drives, breakfast/lunch, internal transfers, basic travel insurance.

**Excluded:** Dinners at some lodges, activities outside safari, optional excursions, premium insurance, visa (not required for EU).

**FAQs:**

- Q: Is malaria a risk? A: Yes in some areas. Addo Famiglia package is malaria-free. Others require antimalarial medication.
- Q: What wildlife will we see? A: Big Five (lion, leopard, buffalo, elephant, rhino) in most seasons. Best viewing June-September.
- Q: Is it safe? A: Yes. Stick to organized tours and avoid isolated areas at night.
- Q: Can we combine safari and Cape Town? A: Yes, all packages include both. Cape Town + Safari is most popular.
- Q: How long are safari drives? A: 3-4 hours each, typically early morning and late afternoon.

### MAROCCO

Rich cultural tapestry: Sahara desert, ancient medinas, Atlas mountains, coastal towns. Closer to Europe, excellent value, deeply authentic experiences.

**Packages:**

- Marrakech + Sahara: €1,800-2,500pp/7-10n - Desert camps, city culture
- Tour Culturale: €2,200pp/10n - Deep dive: Fez, Tangier, Chefchaouen, coast

**Best Season:** March - May, September - November

**Included:** Flights from Malpensa, accommodations (riads and desert camps), breakfast, local guides, camel rides where applicable, basic travel insurance.

**Excluded:** Lunches/dinners except breakfast, activities beyond itinerary, visa (not required for EU), optional excursions, premium insurance.

**FAQs:**

- Q: Is it safe for women? A: Yes. Use common sense (dress modestly in medinas), travel in groups, avoid empty streets late.
- Q: What language? A: Arabic and French are main; English spoken in tourist areas. Guides speak multiple languages.
- Q: Is the Sahara camp comfortable? A: Ranges from basic (nomadic experience) to 3-star luxury camps. Specify preference.
- Q: Can we negotiate prices in markets? A: Yes, haggling is cultural. Our guides show you how.
- Q: What about food? A: Tagines, couscous, Moroccan tea. We note dietary restrictions. Some resorts cater to preferences.

### EGITTO

Ancient civilization meets Red Sea paradise. Nile cruise, pharaonic temples, coral reefs, excellent history and nature combination.

**Packages:**

- Nilo + Sharm: €2,400pp/10n - 5-night Nile cruise, 5 nights Sharm resort
- Nilo + Hurghada: €2,800pp/12n - 6-night Nile cruise, 6 nights Hurghada
- Dahabeya Luxury: €4,500pp/7n - Private sailboat cruise (most intimate)

**Best Season:** October - April

**Included:** Flights from Malpensa, Nile cruise (all meals, excursions, Egyptologist guide), resort accommodation, beach access, basic travel insurance.

**Excluded:** Activities beyond itinerary, optional desert tours, visa (€25 tourist visa on arrival), optional excursions, premium insurance.

**FAQs:**

- Q: How does the Nile cruise work? A: Day excursions to temples (Luxor, Karnak, Abu Simbel), evenings on board with lectures and culture.
- Q: Is malaria a risk? A: No, Nile corridor and Red Sea are malaria-free.
- Q: Can we do snorkeling? A: Yes, excellent in Sharm and Hurghada. Dahabeya stops at coral reefs daily.
- Q: Do we need a visa? A: Yes, €25 Tourist Visa on arrival. Passport must be valid 6 months from return.
- Q: Is it safe for families? A: Very safe. All packages are family-friendly. Dahabeya best for young families.

## 4. GENERAL POLICIES

### Payment Terms

- Deposit: 30% to confirm booking (or 25% if booked more than 90 days in advance)
- Balance: Due 45 days before departure
- Late payments: May forfeit reservation if not received by deadline

### Cancellation Policy

- More than 60 days before departure: €50 administrative fee
- 60-31 days: 25% of total package
- 30-15 days: 50% of total package
- 14-7 days: 75% of total package
- Less than 7 days: 100% (no refund)
- Premium resorts (Singita, COMO Cocoa): Have stricter cancellation terms (100% after 90 days, non-refundable)

### Travel Insurance

- Basic insurance included: €50,000 medical coverage, flight delays, baggage loss
- Optional cancellation insurance: 5-7% of package price (recommended)
- Optional premium repatriation: €120-180 per person (recommended for adventure packages)

### Documents Required

- Passport: Valid for at least 6 months from date of return
- Blank pages: Minimum 2 blank pages in passport
- Visas: See destination FAQs (most EU citizens don't need pre-arranged visas)
- Travel documents: We send itineraries, hotel vouchers, flight confirmations 2 weeks before departure
