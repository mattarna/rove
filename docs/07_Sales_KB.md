## Urgency Patterns (Push if Present)

- Maldive Easter package: Nearly sold out by mid-March; price increases by 10-15% after sold-out
- Safari June-July: Premium lodges book 6-8 weeks in advance; last-minute availability rare
- Thai November-December: Peak season, best dates fill by September
- School holidays (Easter, summer, Christmas): All destinations busy 8-12 weeks ahead
- Flash offers (web-exclusive): Limited slots, expire in 48-72 hours

## Upsell Strategies

- Beach bungalow → Water villa with private pool (Maldive): "+€1,200-2,000 for exclusive access and direct ocean entry"
- Standard safari lodge → Luxury lodge (Sudafrica): "Upgrade to Singita for €4,000 more and experience world-class service"
- Nilo + Sharm → Dahabeya upgrade (Egitto): "Private sailboat with gourmet dining and personalized excursions"
- Island hopping → Luxury Thai add-on (Thailand): "5-star beach club day pass in Phuket; transfers and lunch included"
- Standard insurance → Premium repatriation (all): "Medical emergencies covered up to €50k; repatriation to Italy up to €180pp"

## Objection Handling

### "Too expensive / I found cheaper on Booking / Expedia"

Response: "Booking prices are hotel-only. Our packages include flights from Malpensa, transfers, curated experiences, and an Egyptologist (on Nile cruises) or safari guide. Plus, our team has personally been to every destination - we design your trip, not just book a hotel. Can I show you the value breakdown?"

### "Budget too low for my preference"

Response: "I understand. Let me suggest either a shorter duration at the premium resort you want, or a 3-destination combo at a lower daily rate. Or we could look at our next-tier destination within your budget. What matters most to you - the resort name, the destination itself, or the trip duration?"

### "Can you give me a discount?"

PRICING RULE: Never discount premium packages (Maldive COMO, St. Regis, Four Seasons, Safari Singita/Londolozi). Instead: offer upgrade paths, add free nights, or bundle combo packages. For mid-tier, small discounts (3-5%) only if 60+ days advance or group size >4.

## Closing Techniques

- Assumptive close: "Your deposit secures the dates. What payment method is easiest for you?"
- Limited-time close: "The April Maldive package holds until end of week. Shall I add your names?"
- Choice close: "Would you prefer the 7-night or 10-night Maldive experience?"
- Urgency + value: "Early bird deposit (30%) is due to lock in the price before we raise rates. Shall we proceed?"
